
MerchantTribe
==========================

MerchantTribe is a free open source ecommerce application built on ASP.NET MVC. 
We provide it for free to bring together a community of merchants and web 
professionals. If you find the software useful please join our community at 
MerchantTribe.com. Becoming an active member and recruiting other members 
is the best way to show your thanks for the software.

For licensing information please see the included LICENSE.txt file.

http://MerchantTribe.com
